# DearMe
a journaling app that has been put together for the ease of use and to avoid the big sharks

# why DearMe
- Saves all your journals safely
- easy to use & simple UI
- you can set custom dates
- cool organization according to month
- AI Integration - to tell how to improve your daily life

# features you'll love
- `Daily Journaling` – Capture thoughts, feelings, and moments with a distraction-free editor
- `Weekly AI Reports` – Get thoughtful summaries, patterns, and growth insights from your journals
- `Tags & Organization` – Add tags to entries so you can easily find and group related thoughts
- `Private & Secure` – Your journals are only visible to you
- `Cloud Sync` – Access your memories across devices
- `Clean UI` – Minimal, warm, and non-distracting writing experience

# under the hood?
- Frontend – Next.js (React), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- Backend + Auth – Firebase (Auth + Firestore)
- AI Reports – Hack Club AI (multiple open models)
- Hosting – Vercel
- Styling – Warm minimal UI + subtle gradients
